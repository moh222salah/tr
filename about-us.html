When you book with Falcon Adventure, you’re not just booking a trip, you’re choosing personalised service, expert guidance, and unforgettable experiences. Our professional team are dedicated to making sure every aspect of your trip is enjoyable.

With a blend of British and Egyptian management, we offer the perfect combination of excellent customer service and local expertise. Whether you’re exploring ancient wonders, diving into the Red Sea, or embarking on thrilling desert adventures, we ensure a seamless and stress-free experience. 

If you already know which trips you’d like to do and the dates you’d like to go, we always recommend booking in advance. This helps secure your spot and ensures availability on your preferred dates, especially during busy seasons.

All trips offered by Falcon Adventure include return transfers in an air-conditioned vehicle.

We accept payment in cash once you're in Egypt, and we accept GBP, USD, EUR, and EGP. Payment can be made by card via a secure payment link. 

Children’s prices apply to ages 2 to 11 years (inclusive). Children under 2 can join most of our trips for free.

Once your booking is confirmed, you will receive an email or message with all the details of your trip. We will also reconfirm with you the day before, providing information on what you need, the pick-up time, and the meeting point. If you meet one of our reps at your hotel to book, they will also provide you with a receipt.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, our team is always happy to help.

You can book your trip online, through our social media channels, or by contacting us directly via email or WhatsApp.
